My Story

Hello, my name is Charlie Swinhoe. I'm 25 years old and from Exeter in the South West of England.

I love to surf, skateboard, play golf, and train hard in the gym. I also enjoy traveling, cooking, and reading.

After spending a long time traveling the world, I decided to pursue web development. I was inspired to chase a career that allows me to solve problems, be creative, and have the freedom to continue exploring beautiful places while immersing myself in my interests.

Experience

I have had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects, from creating websites for small businesses such as life coaches, authors, and personal trainers, to developing comprehensive websites for multi-million pound revenue organizations. I handle every aspect of the process independently, from logo and design creation to chatbot integration, e-commerce features, and even copywriting. My goal is to transform ideas into fully functioning web applications, starting from scratch and ensuring a seamless and professional result.
